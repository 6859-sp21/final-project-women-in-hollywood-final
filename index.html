How do we evaluate
the representation of women in movies?

To do this, we use something called the Bechdel Test.

The Bechdel Test (otherwise known as the Bechdel-Wallace Test or the Bechdel Rule) is a simple test which names the following three criteria:

  • (1) it has to have at least two women in it,
  • (2) who talk to each other, about
  • (3) something besides a man.
