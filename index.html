What is the Bechdel Test?

The Bechdel Test, or Bechdel-Wallace Test, sometimes called the Bechdel Rule is a simple test which names the following three criteria:

  • (1) it has to have at least two women in it, who
  • (2) who talk to each other, about
  • (3) something besides a man.

Unfortunately, a lot of big box office hits still don't pass this test. Explore the following visualizations to learn more!
